Shipping & Returns

Where possible, all our deliveries are fulfilled by Royal Mail, Parcelforce or Yodel and we ship throughout the world. The shipping cost for your order will be displayed once address information has been entered, prior to any payment being taken at the online checkout. We calculate the cost of shipping by weight, so please see the table below for a detailed breakdown of our shipping costs.

UK Delivery

 
For UK orders, please allow up to 5 working days for delivery.
Any UK order with an item total value of over £30 is eligible for free shipping and this will be automatically provided at the checkout.


International Delivery Destinations 


We ship parcels to customers throughout the world, wherever possible. If you have any queries or concerns please Contact Us. Please allow up to 10 working days for delivery to Europe, and up to 28 working days for delivery to the rest of the world (Worldwide).

 

Returns Address

If, for any reason, you need to return received goods to us (please read our Terms & Conditions), please let us know within seven working days why you wish to return the item(s) by emailing us at onlineshop@shakespeare.org.uk. We will then be in touch to make suitable arrangements. Please return the package to:

Faulty Goods


If, for any reason, you are not satisfied with the goods you receive, please email us in the first instance, with your details and a summary of the fault to onlineshop@shakespeare.org.uk and we will contact you to make suitable arrangements to either refund the sale or replace the faulty goods.
